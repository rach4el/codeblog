Frequently Asked Questions


Here you'll find answers to our most frequently asked questions!
What are the riding level categories?
All Levels: Suitable for everyone, from beginners to advanced riders.
Beginner: These routes are mostly flat trails with minimal obstacles.
Novice: Routes for those possessing the ability to trot and short canter.
Intermediate: Riders who can easily walk, trot, and gallop over different terrain.
Advanced: For experienced riders, these routes may be physically challenging.


What are the guidelines for posting comments or creating trail routes?
All content must be respectful and relevant. The admin team reviews all submissions.
How can I stay updated on the newest trails?
Our trails are always listed in order of newest first, you can also use our category filter to see the newest posts for your level or area so that way you'll always be presented with the newest posts first!
Can I add comments to a trail post?
Yes, you can. Once logged in, click on a trail post to add your comment. It will be reviewed by the admin before being published.

How can I contact you if I have a question?
Log in or sign up, visit our "Contact" section. Fill in and complete a contact request. Once submitted, you can edit, delete, or view an admin's response.

How can I contribute by posting a new trail route?
Log in or Sign up and visit our "Create" section. Fill in the trail details and submit. The admin team will review your post, if approved it will be posted!
